PID Control of Rotary Inverted Pendulum

This experiment focuses on the design and analysis of a control system for a rotary inverted pendulum in suspended (upright) mode. It uses sensitivity functions to ensure robustness, performance, and stability under varying conditions.

Block Diagram:

Input r(t) parameters:

  • Step Input Magnitude: (0 to 2)
  • Step Time: (0 to 10 sec)

Controller (Kp, Ki, Kd) parameters:

  • Proportional Gain (Kp): (0.1 to 2.5)
  • Integral Gain (Ki): (0.1 to 0.5)
  • Derivative Gain (Kd): (0.25 to 5)

External Disturbance:

  • Magnitude (Md): (0.1 to 0.5)
  • Time (Td): (1.0 to 10.0 sec)

Practical Output

  • Response
  • Rise Time
  • Peak Time
  • Settling Time
  • Steady State Error
